WATER MANAGEMENT & IIRIGATION
GILGIT BALTISTAN

MESSAGE FROM CHIEF MINISTER GILGIT
BALTISTAN
Khalid Khorshid

Water is a critical resource for sustainable economic development of pakistan and in this respective irrigated agriculture is of great importance in the scio-economic life of the country. Water Managment & irrigation Department Giligt Baltistan has a long term vision for irrigation sector to provide adequate, equitable and reliable irrigation supplies to the cultivable lands of Gilgit Baltistan aiming at enhanced agricultural productivity with focus on broad based institutional reforms.

MESSAGE FROM MINISTER IRRIGATION

Water managment and irrigation Department Gilgit Baltistan bringing prosperity to the inhabitants to its hard-working farmers.Over a centery old crumbing infrastructure is stretched to the limit to provide reliable and equitable water supplies of the ever increasing demands of the fram and non-fram sectors. We shall strive to restore the prestig and glory of the Gilgit Baltistan irrigation Department by rehabittating the infrastructure , formatting and implementing comprehensive legislation on water resources, investing in development of our Human Resources and most importantly root out favoritism ans nepotism in the Department.
